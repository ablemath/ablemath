AEC Mission Statement

Able Enrichment Centre is a non-profit, volunteer operated, community organization where students enrich their knowledge, operating skills and interests in mathematics. The main objectives of the centre are; to remove the students' fear of mathematics and to reinforce the students' confidence to master mathematics. The Centre also provides the opportunity for university students to practice their skills in teaching and mentoring younger students.
