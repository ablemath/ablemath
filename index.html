Welcome to the Able Enrichment Center (AEC), a Saturday Mathematics school located in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada.

AEC will be held at St. John's Ravenscourt for the {{ site.registrationdate | date: '%Y' }}-{{ site.registrationdate | date: '%Y' | plus: 1 }} year.

Able Enrichment Centre (AEC) is a non-profit, volunteer operated, community organization where students enrich their knowledge, operating skills and interests in mathematics. The main objectives of the centre are; to remove the students' fear of mathematics and to reinforce the students' confidence to master mathematics. The Centre provides recreational programs, such as line dancing, badminton and basket ball games for parents and friends. The Centre also provides the opportunity for university students to practice their skills in teaching and mentoring younger students. (Read More)
